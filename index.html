About Me

Hello! I am a recent graduate of Appalachian State Univesity in Boone, NC. Here, I majored in biology with a minor in physics and acquired a GIS certficate. As my time at App State came to an end, I discovered my passion for web map development. Since then, I've been constantly working to perfect my skills with coding languages (HTML, CSS, Java) and geospatial software (QGIS, ArcGIS). I am currently based out of Laramie, WY, where I am assisting in research related to the alpine lakes of the Wind River Range, WY. My hobbies include whitewater kayaking, climbing, running, and anything else outside.

I hope to pursue a graduate level education in biology in the near future. In the meantime, I am seeking to improve on both web map design and website development. That said, if you have any interest in letting me build a map (interactive or static) or website for you, please reach out!
